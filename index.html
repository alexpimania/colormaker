About:

The Color Maker is a project that allows you to be able to actually turn dials to make a color of your preference.
This would benefit the average citizen so much. Research suggest that more than 70% percent of some people would rather turn some dials to make a color than use a website.
This is compleatly understandable as through the process of evolution, humans have always been pushing, pulling and turning things and they ahve not been dragging a mouse accross a screen selecting a color!
This is why we the color maker!
