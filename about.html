About the Color Maker






The Color Maker is a piece of electronics that allows you to make a color just by turning any of three dials that correspond to a color. The color maker responds to movements in the dials by firstly, changing the color of the LED accordingly and secondly, by making a noise with a mini speaker on the color maker. The noise is proportional to the average turn amount of the color maker plus the distance from the ultrasonic sensor.


Components:

  • Arduino
  • USB cable
  • Bread board
  • 3x 1K potentiomiters
  • 1K resitor
  • Lots of jumper wires!
